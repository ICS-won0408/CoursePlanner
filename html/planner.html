The Course Planner is an online tool used for planning courses at ICS. It provides information and student ratings for each course.

Requirements

In order to graduate, you must meet the minimum credit requirement, which is 25 credits. In addition to that, you must also have the minimum credit requirement for each type of course:

  • Science: 3.0 credits
  • Math: 3.0 credits
  • Fine & Performing Arts: 3.0 credits
  • Social Science: 3.0 credits
  • Language Arts: 4.0 credits
  • World Languages: 2.0 credits
  • Bible: 1.0 credit per year at ICS
  • Information and Technology Literacy: 0.5 credits
  • Health: 0.5 credits
